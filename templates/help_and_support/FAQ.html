What is City Voting Platform?

City Voting Platform is the best decision for government of a small city to connect with society in order to create better place for all to live in. Using user-friendly interface and a variety of instruments people can vote for different city projects, communicate with each other and much more!

How to propose a project idea?

To propose a project idea, log in to your account, go to the "Propose" page and fill out the form. Make sure your proposal contains enough detail to be considered.

How to vote for existing projects?

After logging in, click on the project on the main page that interests you. You can review the details of each proposal before voting and, once you have made your choice, cast your vote.
